How to be a successful Computer Scientist

What will it take to be a successful engineering student within CS?

To be a successful CS student you need a multitide of attributes.

  • Problem solving skills/analytical skills
  • Teamwork/cooperation
  • Communication
  • Internships
  • Learn multiple programming languages
  • List past projects on resumes
  • Presentation Skills
  • Networking Skills
  • Creating new projects to improve skills
  • Math Skills
  • Research
  • Technological Skills

What are some of the attributes of successful students in CS?

  • Passion
  • Dedication
  • Creative
  • Team Player
  • Time-Management Skills
  • Resourceful
  • People Skills
  • Organizational Skills
  • Analytical Thinker

What are the different strategies and pathways to success in CS?

  • Bachelos Degree
  • Masters Degree
  • Minoring in CS and major in a different field
  • Specialization
  • Online Bootcamps
  • Coding websites
  • Certificates
  • Ph.D
  • Internships/Co-ops
