Reflection

Earlier this semester, you wrote a short essay on why you decided to become an engineer.  Reflect back on that essay - how has your vision changed?  Why choose engineering?

Vision

Reflecting on my essay, a few things have changed. In my essay, I mentioned being undecided on what I want to specialize in with a computer science degree. I have decided to specialization in cyber security/IT.

Why choose engineering?

I chose engineering because it's the future. Companies everywhere need a computer scientist because we're relying more on technology then ever before. I enjoy problem solving and technology, so computer science would be a great career for me. Engineering is about problem solving to make the world a better place.

Level of Interest

What excites me the most is getting to change the world through technology. When you work in tech you have to stay up to date with every technological advancement. As a computer scientist I will constantly be learning. I'm interested in creating pratical solutions.
